Saint-Petersburg, Russia

Gazprom Neft Corporate Sales is a marketing operator for Gazprom Neft motor fuels and a stable supplier of high-quality motor fuels to enterprises, small wholesale buyers, and commercial vehicle fleets.

In 2018, the OPTI 24 digital platform was created to sell fuel to corporate customers. The platform includes a service card for obtaining fuel and services, a specialized network of OPTI 24 filling stations for heavy vehicles, as well as an online fuel cost management system. Digital solutions on the OPTI 24 platform allow you to quickly and conveniently top-up driver's accounts 24/7, build routes through gas stations, take into account actual fuel prices for the clients, exchange electronic documents, and others.

The OPTI 24 service card acceptance network unites more than 4,300 Gazpromneft gas stations, partner stations, OPTI 24 gas stations, as well as more than 350 car washes, tire shops, and service stations in 77 regions of Russia and 8 neighboring countries.

In 2020, the number of OPTI 24 users exceeded 42 thousand companies in the regions of Russia and the CIS, including logistics and transport companies, retail chains, cargo carriers, and taxi services. More than 500,000 drivers of commercial vehicles use the OPTI 24 card every month.

To serve such a large number of clients, the Digital Marketing Department has been promoting the digital services of Opti 24 through the high-level performance of the duties of SMM Manager, Product Manager, Design Manager, KPI Metric Analyst, PR Department, and other stakeholders.

The company has a highly developed internal process automation system. In addition, it was necessary to coordinate everything with the Security Service, which required countless data checks and the provision of certificates.

Professional Experience

My last job was amazing. I was responsible for all the budget money of the digital marketing department, analyzed suppliers in the advertising market, invited them to tenders, making and signed contracts with the most suitable ones.

Of course, this all was with the permission of many other departments and thanks to the use of a lot of automated systems such as CRM, electronic document management, automated bill payment, and financial transactions.

My role was not only to smoothly build all routine processes. It was daily / weekly / monthly / quarterly / yearly processes. My role was also in the realization of what the business (in fact top-managers) requires, what the advertising market requires.

I also needed to know how the back office works. With its hard deadlines, a lot of people who must approve every small action, everything was checked by the security service - this is a third part with which I supposed to have special skills for negotiations.

It was important to be able to reach an agreement and set deadlines for my duties. And the difficulty was to demand from colleagues who are not subordinate to me and even who are top managers of the whole company.

During this time, I have successfully carried out the negotiation and contracting processes in providing advertising for a network of gas stations among corporate transport clients. Provided coordination of contract execution and accounting processes. I managed to slash costs of the top management operating activities by 15% due to the centralization of reporting in project management information flow while continuing the expansion of the documentary flow in the back office.

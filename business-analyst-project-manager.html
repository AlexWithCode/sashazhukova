Business Analyst & Project Manager

PRAUD Group Ltd., Consulting Company

May 2015 - November 2016

Saint-Petersburg, Russia

I was really tragic to receive a reduction in my Project Administrator position at a clothing factory BTC Group due to the Currency crisis in Russia at the turn of 2014-2015. After 4 months of searching for a new job, I was invited as a Business Analyst of document flow to the PRAUD Group Consulting Ltd.

The PRAUD Group Company, consisting of two branches in Moscow and St. Petersburg, carried out about 20 projects for the implementation and supervision of automated control systems in the largest manufacturing companies in Russia.

My boss Konstantin Komaedov asked me to analyze and propose solutions for building Project Management and Sales Management processes. Moreover, he experienced a lack of information in the Document Flow process, in control over contracts execution and accounts transactions.

Proficient in:

  • Ability to develop and maintain processes, Desk Top Procedure and Knowledge base articles;

  • Highly collaboration, building on the work of others, and supporting the success of others;

  • Strong Project Management skills, Influencing skills;

  • End-to-end process understanding;

  • Introduction of new ideas.

Professional Experience

In addition to assisting the Committee of Directors in administering office life support, I have developed Project Management tools and a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system.

After successful testing of the Project Management and Sales processes, the developer Aleksey Uryadnikov and I automated and implemented them into the internal management 1C:Enterprise system.

Every week I took part in meetings with project leaders and sales managers where they reported to the CEO. Between meetings, I monitored the execution of tasks for the project teams and the sales team using the automated management online systems I created.

To improve the efficiency of the workflow process and control transactions on the accounts of the group of companies, I analyzed the existing system for coordinating and organizing documents and paying bills. While I was doing the analysis, I was a little shocked by how much time managers spent while calling me with a request to send a copy of the contract or even physically came to the document archive to read the documents.

For me, the lack of automation of the system for introducing the CEO with documents was obvious, since it took him a lot of time without some system. Within 9 months, I divided the entire archive of contracts, about 1000 different documents, according to areas of activity and entered all the documents into the electronic document flow according to the system. Thanks to this, all stakeholders received access to all documents directly from their workplace.

Main Tasks and Responsibilities:

  • Providing recommendations on data administration.

  • Understanding and communicating the financial and operational impact of any changes.

  • Thoroughly studying the business needs of project teams, evaluating these and making recommendations, and supporting the creation of strong business solutions.

  • Collaborate with functional teams (Development, Legal Department, Maintenance) and other involved people to support overall program delivery.

A Difficult Task I Faced With And How I Addressed It

In one of my previous positions as a Project Administrator, I was responsible for automating the Project Management and Selling Management systems that I developed for this company.

The goal was to complete the transition to the new management system by the end of the year, without allowing the new working environment to affect project deadlines.

To do this, I needed to very quickly acquire a new skill for me in describing processes. So, I took the documentation on automation of a similar process of Document Flow to describe the Project Management and Selling Management processes by analogy. I allocated an hour a day for describing the documentation and half an hour a week for a conversation with developers.

As a result, the automation was completed on time. This gave me an idea of where to go in unfamiliar activities while continuing to carry out my usual tasks.
